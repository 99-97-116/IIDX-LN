IIDX LN

This scope of this table is charts that are similar to the Charge Note style in IIDX. This is for players who would like to play that kind of LN chart without the mess of "standard" LN patterning from other games like osu!mania and o2jam.

Type of patterns that you can expect:

  • Short LN that can be hit like regular notes (EROICA)
  • Single LN with notes surrounding it--two LN is acceptable in higher levels (Almagest, Timepiece phase II)
  • Technical LN patterns while nothing else is happening around them(Rejection Girl)
  • Hybrid charts with harsh density spike outside of LN section(s) (栄冠のカンパネラ, INITIATION, NZM)

Combinations of these styles are suitable for the table, but super LN patterns should be avoided.

Preliminary ratings are as follows:
  • -2 covers up to ★6
  • -1 ★7~★13
  • 0 ★14~★20
  • 1, 2,... = ★★1, ★★2,...
